Gone Camping

Gear will make your life easier, along with some solid clothing that protects you from the cold, wind and also predators that may try to take you down. Later on, you'll even be able to craft your own gear to let others know not to mess with you.

While you're searching for a place to stay and getting to know the area, don't forget to regularly pick up wood. You easily get cold so fires are your best friend!

In terms of what you take with you... Just take the essentials. Heavy backpacks slow you down and can eventually get you killed.

Shelter... If you want to survive in the long term, this is something you'll need to look out for early on! Just watch out that you don't enter bears' shelters as they might look as a safe place to stay... They won't be empty forever.
